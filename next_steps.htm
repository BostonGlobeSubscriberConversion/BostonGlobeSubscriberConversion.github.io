Next Steps

Survival analysis might be applied as an extension by including the number of days a visitor takes to convert in our model. Such model enables us predicting the occurring time of a possible subscription.

Retention model is also a reasonable consideration since we have much more observations of a subscriber in our system than a non-subscriber. We may take those advantages to analyze who will stop subscribing/visiting after a period of time.
